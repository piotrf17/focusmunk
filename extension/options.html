Blocked regexes, one per line. The regex must match the /full/ website address, including "https". For example: 

facebook.com

will likely not do what you want. Instead, try:

https://.*\.facebook\.com.*

Allowed regexes, one per line. Useful for allowing part of a website, but blocking the rest. A match on the allowed regexes always lets a website through, even if it matches an entry in the blocked regexes.
